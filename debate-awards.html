International Harvard University Debate Tournament
Octofinalist (Top 16 Debater)

Awarded by Harvard University - Cambridge, MA.

  • Award Date: February 2020
  • Awarded By: Harvard University

I was an octofinalist at the 2020 Harvard University International Debate Tournament. The tournament is one of the most prestigious debate competitions in the world. I debated the resolution "Resolved: The United States should replace Means-Tested Welfare with a Universal Basic Income (UBI)." I effectively used both complex logical argumentation and rhetoric to effectively convince judges while debating both sides of the resolution. Final Record: 6-1.
